Business plan is the compass for doing business. It will help to map out a new course, and navigate through stages of business. In its simplest form, a business plan is a guide a roadmap for your business that outlines goals and details how you plan to achieve those goals.


Here s a list of items in a Business-Plan:

  • Executive Summary
  • Company Overview
  • Products and Services
  • Target Marketing
  • Marketing and Sales Plan
  • Milestones and Metrics
  • Management Team
  • Financial Plan

Three aspects of B-Plan

  • Keep it short

    Business plans should be short and concise. An extremely lengthy business plan is a hassle to deal with, and guarantees that your plan will be demoted.


  • Know your audience

    Business plans should be written in a language that your audience will understand. Accommodate your investors, and keep explanations of your product simple and direct, using terms that everyone can understand.


  • Dont be intimidated

    The majority of business owners and entrepreneurs arent business experts. If you know your business and are passionate about it, writing a business plan and then leveraging your plan for growth will be easy.
